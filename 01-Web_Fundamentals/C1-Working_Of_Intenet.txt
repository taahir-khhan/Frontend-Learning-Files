Network :- 
      - A network is a group of computers or other devices which are connected to each other with the intention of communication.

Internet :- 
      - Short for Interconnected networks, And also known as network of networks
      
      - Millions of of small networks are connected with each other, which empowers the whole internet, that's why it is known as network of networks.
      
Working :- 
      - At a high level, the internet works by connecting millions of devices and computer systems together using a set of 
      standardized protocols, through wired/wireless medium, to transfer the information with each other.

      - These protocols define how information is exchanged between devices and ensure that data is transmitted reliably and securely. 
      Ex- TCP, IP, HTTP etc.
